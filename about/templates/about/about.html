About Honey Shop

" We care about bees and we care about you ! "

Passion for bees

Almost 20 years ago we discovered the passion for bees that would take us from the urban landscape to the quirky mountain town and its surroundings.

This is a business that sells bee products, but in the meantime you really care about bees.

All started when the family, some of us from family, changed their diets, replacing white refined sugar in their recipes with raw honey instead.

Passion for honey

This passion for honey in the kitchen blossomed into an interest in bees and pollinators in general.

Born out of a passion for honey, a curiosity for bees, a love of cooking, and a yearning for connection to community.

Honey Shop sells between 25-35 types of honey depending on the time of year and availability.

Passion for you

Acacia honey is our forever favorite, partly due to the incredible taste, but also because has traveled all the way from Transylvania, Romania.

We are steadfast in our commitment to education and honey bee habitat protection.

Even the landscaping and gardening around our home has bees and other pollinators in mind.
